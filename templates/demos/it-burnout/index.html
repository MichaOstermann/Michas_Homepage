üí° IT-Admin Survival Tips

How to stay (barely) sane in an insane job

01

Document Everything

When (not if) things blow up and management asks "Why didn't we prevent this?", you'll have proof that you warned them 47 times and all budget requests were denied.

02

Automate or Die

You can't manually manage 500 users. Write scripts. Learn PowerShell, Python, Bash. Your sanity depends on it.

03

Set Boundaries

"Not my circus, not my monkeys." Learn to say no. You're not responsible for fixing users' personal laptops, home WiFi, and their smart toaster.

04

Coffee is Life

Invest in a good coffee machine. Your productivity is directly correlated to your caffeine intake. This is scientifically proven*. (*by IT admins)

05

CYA Protocol

Cover Your Ass. Email confirmations for everything. "As per my previous email..." becomes your best friend.

06

Find Your Tribe

Join IT forums, Reddit's /r/sysadmin, Discord servers. Vent to people who understand. You're not alone in this hell.
